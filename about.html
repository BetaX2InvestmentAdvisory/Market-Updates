About Us

Here at Beta x2 Investment Advisory, we aim to provide our insights on different stocks in the US market and provide analysis of the latest market movement. Currently we've just started our venture to become a stock analysis website but in the future, we seek for clients to invest their saving with us.

Our Origin

Since high school, our founder, Johnson Lee has played mutiple 10 week stock market simulations for business class. Ever since turning 18 and being able to open a TFSA to invest in the stock market, Johnson started applying his knowledge and experience learnt from playing the stock market simulation to his TFSA portfolio. The purpose of this project / blog is to track the journey and document Johnson's analysis of the market to see if it turns out to be what is analyzed.
